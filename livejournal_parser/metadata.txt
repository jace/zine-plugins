Version: 0.2
Description: This plugin parses LiveJournal markup. It converts newlines into\
 line break tags; converts &lt;lj user="foo"&gt; tags to links to their\
 LiveJournal pages; avoids converting markup within <lj-raw> sections; and \
 converts up to the first <lj-cut> tag into a Zine <intro> section.
